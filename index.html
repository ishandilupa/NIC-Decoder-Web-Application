How It Works

Old Format

9 digits + V or X letter (e.g., 850421234V)

  • Digits 1–2: Year of birth (19XX)
  • Digits 3–5: Day of year (add 500 for Female)
  • Digits 6–9: Serial number
  • Letter: V = Eligible to vote · X = Not eligible

New Format

12 digits only (e.g., 198504210034)

  • Digits 1–4: Full year of birth
  • Digits 5–7: Day of year (add 500 for Female)
  • Digits 8–11: Serial number
  • Digit 12: Check digit
